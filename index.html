How it works

Three actions. No new desktop to learn.

  1. 01

    Hold and drag

    Hold your docking modifier and drag a window toward any monitor edge.

  2. 02

    Release to park

    The window moves beyond the edge and leaves a colored pull tab behind.

  3. 03

    Peek or restore

    Hover to preview, click to restore, or double-click a stack to open Window Shuffle.

Built to give windows back

Recovery is not an afterthought.

EdgeDock records original positions before parking. Pausing or exiting restores managed windows, and emergency recovery bypasses animation.

  • Crash-safe recovery journal
  • Instant emergency shortcut
  • Display disconnect and rotation handling
  • Reduced motion and larger tab targets
  • Settings stored locally on your PC
